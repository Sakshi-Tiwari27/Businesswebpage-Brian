The Course

The Turning Retirement into Desirement Course provides prospective and current retirees a method of identifying their skills and capabilities and a workable structure to identify the opportunities and activities available to them. The course provides:

  • Navigating the emotional factors impacting on modern retirees.
  • Explores and expands on what is important to you in retirement.
  • Provides a practical and simple methodology to identify your skills and capabilities.
  • Delivers a comprehensive exploration of what is available to you in retirement.
  • Employs a specifically developed electronic process for the aligning of your skills and interests with desirable activities in your retirement.
  • Presents a comprehensive guide which clearly outlines a practical process for achieving your goals
  • Constructs an overview of what your future retirement will look like.

“Whilst many courses available deal with the financial side of retirement and others try to address the emotional impact, few if any provide a practical, tangible workable framework to not only prepare you for retirement but provide the tools that will empower you to ensure your retirement is everything you dreamed of. I have specifically designed this course for those that not only want to remove the angst associated with retiring but be empowered with the ability to turn their retirement into a life they dreamed of.”


Brian Clarke
MMan&Lead, GDipMan&Lead, GCAppMan, AssocDipSS, CertIVTrain&Ass

In addition, the course provides you with all the tangible tools needed to ensure your retirement is productive and rewarding. You receive:

A comprehensive book titled “Turning Retirement into Desirement” which provides a quantifiable blueprint for you to follow on your road to fulfillment.

Electronic access to the digital program “The Perfect Desirement Creator” which immediately identifies multiple activities which complement your skills and interests.

A step-by-step adaptable Instigation Plan which incorporates networking, self-marketing and engagement needs to ensure a logical and practical approach to achieving your dreams
